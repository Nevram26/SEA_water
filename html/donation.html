Support NGOs Working on SDG 6

We've selected a number of reputable NGOs that are working tirelessly to ensure clean water and proper sanitation for communities in need. Each of these organizations has a transparent and effective approach to addressing water-related challenges.

Planet Water Foundation's Impact in the Philippines

Planet Water has been a vital force in providing clean water to countless Filipinos. Since 2010, they've installed over 410 water filtration systems, serving over 830,000 people. These systems are particularly beneficial in schools, where they've been paired with water health and hygiene education programs.

The Manila Water Foundation

The Manila Water Foundation is a non-profit organization that provides water, sanitation, and hygiene services to marginalized communities in the Philippines. They work with communities to implement sustainable WASH systems. The foundation has had a positive impact on the communities they serve.

water.org

The Water Organization is a non-profit organization that provides water, sanitation, and hygiene services to marginalized communities in the Philippines. They work with communities to develop sustainable solutions that meet their needs. The foundation has had a positive impact on the communities they serve, and they are committed to continuing their work.
