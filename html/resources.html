UN Sustainable Development Goal 6 Overview

A comprehensive breakdown of SDG 6 by the United Nations, including targets, indicators, and progress updates.

World Health Organization (WHO) – Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH)

Learn about global health efforts to improve water, sanitation, and hygiene, including guidelines and reports on water quality and sanitation in healthcare facilities and communities.

UNICEF – Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

UNICEF works to ensure every child has access to safe drinking water, sanitation, and hygiene. Explore their initiatives and get in-depth data on the global WASH situation.

World Water Day (UN-Water)

World Water Day is an annual event that highlights the importance of freshwater and advocates for sustainable management of water resources. Explore key facts, reports, and resources related to SDG 6.

Global Water Partnership

The Global Water Partnership (GWP) brings together various stakeholders to ensure water security. Their resources include policy papers, tools, and case studies on water management.

WaterAid

WaterAid is a global organization dedicated to providing clean water, toilets, and hygiene education to underserved communities. Their website offers insightful reports and stories about ongoing efforts.

The Water Project

This nonprofit focuses on providing reliable water solutions to communities in sub-Saharan Africa. Learn more about their projects and how you can get involved.

National Geographic – Water Crisis Explained

National Geographic offers an in-depth look at the causes, effects, and potential solutions to the global water crisis.

SDG Tracker – SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

This platform provides up-to-date data on how countries are progressing toward achieving SDG 6. You can explore visual data representations and country-specific insights.

Water Footprint Network

A platform for understanding and managing water use and its environmental impact. Their resources include data on global water consumption and recommendations for reducing water footprints.
